Clay application
for different skin types

Dry skin

First apply cosmetic oil (natural), and then apply clay in an even layer for 5-10 minutes. Wash off with warm water

Normal skin

Apply the clay evenly for 10 minutes. Wash off with warm water

Oily skin

First apply cosmetic oil (natural), and then apply clay in an even layer for 5-10 minutes. Wash off with warm water.

Combination skin

Apply the clay locally to specific areas of the skin for 10-15 minutes. Wash off with warm water

In modern cosmetology, such clay used in as hot compress, cosmetic masks or baths . It is also added to various cosmetics.
